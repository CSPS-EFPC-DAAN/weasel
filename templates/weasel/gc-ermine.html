Why should I care?

This is a new way to engage with the government of canada and the public service. It's not meant to replace anything. It's meant to support everything. It doesn't want to own anything, it wants to ask others what they think and do its best to tell the human the reply. its like a search engine. its like an alexa. its like your colleague you always ask for that link to that file. its a way to watch the house of commons. its a way to get your T4 form. its a way to find the path make canada your home.

How does this work?

It all starts with a question with intent. You can speak/type this into the weasel. It takes your words and through an AI framework figures out what you meant. Then it takes that intent and the bits it found and searches against it's know how. If it knows what to do it just does it. You want your T4? Here's the download page from CRA. You want to see your government in action on climate change. Here's CPAC for the recent session.

What Weasel recognizes and what it knows will only grow with time.
